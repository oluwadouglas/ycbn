About YCBF

Empowering Youth, Building Communities, Creating Change

The Youth Capacity Building Forum is dedicated to empowering young Ugandans with the skills, knowledge, and opportunities they need to become leaders in their communities. Through innovative programs and collaborative partnerships, we create pathways to success.

Our Program Categories

Three Core Areas of Impact

Skills Development Programs

Comprehensive training in digital literacy, entrepreneurship, leadership, and technical skills to prepare youth for the modern workforce.

Community Project Support

Supporting youth-led initiatives that address local challenges through funding, mentorship, and resource mobilization.

Mentorship & Networking

Connecting young leaders with experienced mentors and building networks for collaboration and career advancement.

  • Providing essential training in leadership and entrepreneurship
  • Offering mentorship and networking opportunities
  • Facilitating access to funding and resources for youth-led projects
